Many are required for basic use of MatCal and a few are optional for use 
with specific features. For the use of all features, the following python 
packages are required to be installed by users in addition to 
the MatCal install. For convenience, the packages and their licenses 
are listed below. Note that while we attempt to keep this up-to-date, errors
may exist. Summaries of these different licenses are at the bottom of this file.
